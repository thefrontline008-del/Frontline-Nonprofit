Meet Our Team

Priya Maydipalle

President

I am a rising senior at Westborough High School. I am passionate about dancing, spending most of my childhood performing in the Boston Ballet and Triveni’s School of Karnatic Dance. After graduation, I wish to pursue a career in finance. I am dedicated to this mission because I believe true progress comes from rebuilding lives even through the smallest acts.

Hasini Garrepalli

Treasurer

I am a rising junior at Franklin High School. I am passionate about music, especially singing, and mentor kids with voice lessons throughout the year. I also enjoy dancing and performing. I am dedicated to this mission because I believe my time can uplift others and make a positive difference in my community.

Yashu Kukula

Secretary

I am part of the varsity tennis team and I volunteer at the food pantry on weekends.
